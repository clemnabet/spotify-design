Design means something slightly different no matter where you work. Here at Spotify, we're all about collaboration and working together to bring music to everyone. If you are interested in joining us, take a look at our open roles and see if you're a fit.

Design

Designers develop strategies with their product partners using insights about real people, and they craft these insights into beautiful products. At Spotify, designers are passionate about how it works and how it feels.

Data-science

As a Data Scientist, your mission is to turn terabytes of data into insights. You will study user behaviour, strategic initiatives, markets, content, and new features and bring data and insights into every decision we make. Above all, your work will impact the way the world experiences music.

User Research

We need you to help us define, plan and conduct user research. Your role will be to deliver compelling insights to our teams. You'll contribute to the growth of our evidence-based product and provide rationale for design decisions..

Design Operations

Our Design Ops team is pretty new. We're looking out for superstar ops humans to join our team in 2019. If you love people, design, and process and are keen to help us carve out what Design Ops means at Spotify, then send us an email and introduce yourself.

UX Writing

UX Writing is part of design. We help shape the entire user experience by providing the right message at the right time. We do much more than write the words. Our work includes content strategy, information architecture, naming, guidelines, and much more. We look for UX writers who are passionate about creating great user experience.

Design Prototyping

A unique part of the design organization, we’re a group of designer/developer hybrids focused on bringing design concepts to life. We work with teams across Spotify to create prototypes that aid in the design, vetting, and user-testing of new product features. We don't have any open positions at the moment but check back soon.
